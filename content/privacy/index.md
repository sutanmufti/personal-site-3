This policy explains what data is collected when you visit sutan.co.uk and how it is used.

## Who I Am

This website is operated by Sutan Mufti. For any privacy-related enquiries, contact me at [upsutan@gmail.com](mailto:upsutan@gmail.com).

## What Data Is Collected

This site uses **Google Analytics 4** to understand how visitors interact with its content. If you accept cookies, Google Analytics may collect:

- Pages visited and time spent on each page
- General geographic location (country or city level)
- Device type, operating system, and browser
- Referral source (how you arrived at the site)

No names, email addresses, or other personally identifiable information are collected through analytics. Google Analytics 4 anonymises IP addresses by default, so your full IP address is never stored.

## Legal Basis

Data is processed only with your consent. You are asked for your consent when you first visit the site. You may withdraw consent at any time by clearing your browser's local storage for this site or by declining cookies on your next visit.

## Google Analytics and Consent Mode

This site implements **Google Consent Mode v2**. Until you accept cookies, all analytics signals are set to denied. Google Analytics will not track your visit unless you explicitly consent.

Google processes analytics data as a data processor on behalf of this site. For details on how Google handles data, refer to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## Data Retention

Analytics data is retained for 14 months in Google Analytics, after which it is automatically deleted.

## Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

- Access the personal data held about you
- Request correction or deletion of your data
- Withdraw consent at any time
- Lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at [ico.org.uk](https://ico.org.uk)

## Cookies and Withdrawing Consent

This site uses browser local storage (not HTTP cookies) to remember your consent preference under the key `cookie_consent`. No tracking cookies are set before you give consent.

To withdraw consent and reset your preference at any time:

1. Open your browser's DevTools (F12 or right-click → Inspect).
2. Go to the **Application** tab (Chrome/Edge) or **Storage** tab (Firefox).
3. Open **Local Storage** and select `https://sutan.co.uk`.
4. Find the key `cookie_consent` and delete it.
5. Reload the page. The consent banner will reappear and no analytics data will be collected until you choose again.

Alternatively, clearing all site data for sutan.co.uk in your browser settings will have the same effect.

## International Data Transfers

Google Analytics may transfer data to Google's servers located outside the UK and European Economic Area, including the United States. Google relies on Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the UK Information Commissioner's Office to ensure that data transferred internationally receives an equivalent level of protection.

## Third-Party Services

Other than Google Analytics (when consented), this site does not use any third-party tracking, advertising, or data-sharing services.
